About Me

Putting myself into words is practically a hellish task for me to conquer. I mean hell I put off doing this till last minute. But...I've come across a term for myself. A funny one. A Swiss Army Knife of Creativity. Doing a ton of creative work in different disciplines from Instrumentation, Mixing, 3D Art, Photography, Cooking, Abstract Art, Programming, Game Development, Generative Art. You can see why I'm a lil bit of an enigma of sorts.

I've been paleo and keto for 8 years now - which means I dont eat sugar, bread, milk, sweets, etc. All of what I own fits into a car. My purpose of life is improving/inspiring myself and others through the magic of creativity.

My work is especially focused in the domain of Sound. Mastering, Mixing, Composition, Sound Design, Guitar playing, Bass, Piano, Sampling, Generative Music, Ambient, Any genre you name. I love the world of audio and sound and dedicate my life to it.
